About Me

I live in Orlando, FL where I am a MERN full stack web developer for Wise Brothers, Inc. I enjoy the problem solving and continuous learning inherent in web development. I prefer working with the back-end and databases most.

While I am most familiar with the MERN stack, I also know PHP and SQL. My favorite languages/libraries are React.js for the front end and MongoDB for a database. I prefer Node.js with Express for the backend.

I have two pet cats, Pablo and Chicken. I enjoy reading, coding, and writing my epic fantasy novel in my spare time.
